Full description:
This app adds St Albert Transit buses information to MonTransit.

This app provides the bus schedule.

StAT buses serve St Albert, Edmonton in Alberta, Canada.

Once this application is installed, the MonTransit app will display buses information (schedule...).

This application only has a temporary icon and no screen: download the MonTransit app (free) in the "More ..." section bellow or by following this Google Play link http://goo.gl/pCk5mV

You can install this application on the SD card but it is not recommended.

The information comes from the GTFS file provided by StAT (St Albert Transit).
http://stalbert.ca/getting-around/stat-transit/rider-tools/open-data-gtfs/

This application is free and open-source:
https://github.com/mtransitapps/ca-st-albert-transit-bus-android

This app is not related with StAT (St Albert Transit) and the City of St. Albert.
